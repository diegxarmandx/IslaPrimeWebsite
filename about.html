About Us

Committed to Puerto Rico’s Progress

Building Solid Infrastructure with Real Impact

At Isla Prime Logistics, we combine expertise, innovation, and commitment to deliver infrastructure and transport solutions focused on water supply systems. We proudly serve both the public and private sectors, ensuring each project contributes to the well-being of Puerto Rico’s communities.

Since our beginning, we’ve worked with purpose and precision to become a trusted and efficient company. Our team is made up of passionate professionals dedicated to building a stronger, more resilient Puerto Rico—one project at a time.
