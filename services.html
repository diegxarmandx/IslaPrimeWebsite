Our Services

Explore how Isla Prime Logistics can support your construction and transport needs.

Reliable Construction & Transport Solutions for Puerto Rico’s Progress

At Isla Prime Logistics, we specialize in delivering top-tier services in infrastructure construction—particularly water supply systems—and heavy transport solutions. Our mission is to help both public and private sectors build safer, stronger, and more sustainable communities across the island.

Water Infrastructure Construction

We lead the development of essential water distribution systems and underground pipe installations for municipalities and private projects. From planning to execution, we ensure each job meets the highest standards of safety and quality.

  • Potable water systems
  • Sanitary and stormwater pipe installations
  • Trenching and backfilling
  • Valve & meter installation

Heavy Transport Services

We offer reliable hauling and delivery services for construction materials, machinery, and supplies across Puerto Rico.

  • Delivery of pipe, concrete, and gravel
  • Equipment relocation
  • Route logistics for large-scale projects

Site Preparation & Excavation

Our team provides land preparation, excavation, grading, and leveling services to ensure that each site is ready for solid construction.

  • Earthmoving and clearing
  • Site layout and planning
  • Elevation grading and compacting
  • Soil hauling and dumping

Project Management & Consulting

We work with government agencies and developers to provide full construction oversight and strategic support to bring your infrastructure projects to completion—on time and on budget.

  • Planning and scheduling
  • Budget estimation
  • Contractor and crew supervision
  • Compliance and documentation
